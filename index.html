Don't Forget The Crystals!

Uncle Rico is at it again. He was unable to get his time traveling machine to work.

By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score however you will not know the value till you click on the crystal.

Every new round, the values of the crystals will change.

You win by matching the total score to random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Help Uncle Rico out by adding the correct amount of crystals!
